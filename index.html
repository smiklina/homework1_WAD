Sept 27, 2022

The Delta Career Day brings together employers and students of the Institute of Computer Science, Institute of Mathematics and Statistics and the School of Economics and Business Administration. At the event, companies can offer traineeship and promote job opportunities, as well as build their reputation as an employer. Students in turn get an idea of which companies are looking for employees, where to find a good traineeship placement and where they might work in the future.

March 10, 2022

University of Tartu Institute of Computer Science would like to invite you to take part in 19th Estonian Summer School on Computer and Systems Science (ESSCaSS 2022)

The international summer school welcomes all PhD students, MSc students interested in research, postdocs, faculty members and representatives from industry. School will traditionally cover modern research directions in computer science, systems science, software engineering, artificial intelligence, and any other related disciplines like bioinformatics, neuroscience, or likes. The main focus of this year's ESSCaSS is on machine learning, responsible AI, calibration and fairness of machine learning, etc.
